// About Me

I'm a sound designer with over 10 years of experience creating immersive and engaging sonic landscapes. My passion is transforming abstract concepts into auditory experiences that evoke emotions and tell stories.

I've worked with video game development studios, independent filmmakers, and international brands to create unique and memorable sonic identities.
